Deliver Early, Learn Often

Traditional

Plan everything → Build everything → Deliver once at the end

Feedback comes too late to act on.

Incremental

Plan a little → Build a little → Deliver → Learn → Repeat

Each increment is usable and gets real feedback.

Scrum delivers working product frequently — so you can learn and adapt before it’s too late.

2-3 min. Transition: "So what exactly is the thing you're delivering?"
